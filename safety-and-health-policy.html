Safety And Health Policy

Agasti Safety And Health Policy

Introduction At Agasti, we prioritize the health and safety of our employees, contractors, and all stakeholders involved in our operations. As a responsible organization, we are committed to maintaining a safe, healthy, and secure working environment in line with all applicable laws and industry standards.

Policy Statement Agasti is committed to:

  1. 1. Ensuring Safe Working Conditions: We will maintain a work environment that meets or exceeds health and safety regulations, proactively identifying and mitigating potential hazards.

  2. 2. Employee Health & Well-being: We prioritize the physical and mental health of all employees, promoting practices that support work-life balance, stress management, and wellness initiatives.

  3. 3. Compliance with Legal Requirements: We will comply with all relevant health and safety regulations, standards, and guidelines applicable to our operations.

  4. 4. Training and Awareness: We will ensure all employees and contractors receive appropriate training, knowledge, and support to perform their duties safely and efficiently.

  5. 5. Accident Prevention: We will actively work to prevent workplace accidents and incidents through a combination of hazard identification, risk assessments, and preventive measures.

  6. 6. Emergency Preparedness: We will maintain emergency response plans and ensure employees are trained to handle emergencies effectively, minimizing risk to life and property.

  7. 7. Ongoing Monitoring and Improvement: We are committed to continuously monitoring our safety practices and improving them through regular audits, reviews, and feedback.

Responsibilities

  • Management: Ensures that health and safety policies are effectively implemented and updated regularly. Management is also responsible for setting safety objectives and ensuring compliance.

  • Employees: Each employee is responsible for following safety protocols, attending training sessions, and reporting any hazards or unsafe conditions.
