Secondary Seals / Weather Shield / Wiper Seal /Tank Seal / Roof Seal Manufacturer and Exporter

We “Agasti Tank Works Private Limited “one of the Leading Manufacturer and Exporter Various Types of Secondary Seals / Weather Shield / Wiper Seal / Wiper Tip Seal / Gasketed Type Secondary Seal / Secondary Compression Plate Seal / Tank Seal / Roof Seal for Floating Roof Oil Storage Tanks in India.

The Agasti Make Secondary Seals for Floating Roof Oil Storage Tanks are designed to complement primary seals by providing an additional layer of vapour control and enhancing the overall efficiency and safety of the storage system. Here’s a detailed overview:

Product Overview:

Agasti Make Secondary Seals are engineered to provide an effective secondary barrier to vapour emissions in floating roof tanks, working in conjunction with primary seals to ensure maximum containment of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other emissions.

Key Features:

  • Enhanced Vapor Control: The secondary seal provides an additional layer of protection, significantly reducing vapor losses.
  • Durable Construction: Made from high-quality, corrosion-resistant materials that can withstand harsh environmental conditions and prolonged exposure to stored products.
  • Compatibility: Designed to work seamlessly with various types of primary seals, including both liquid and vapor mounted primary foam seals.
  • Easy Installation and Maintenance: Engineered for straight forward installation with minimal maintenance requirements, helping to reduce operational downtime.
  • Compliance with Industry Standards: Meets or exceeds environmental regulations and industry standards for vapor control in oil storage tanks.

 

Benefits:

  • Significant Emission Reduction: By providing a secondary barrier, these seals help in further reducing the release of VOCs, contributing to better air quality and environmental protection.
  • Enhanced Safety: Reduces the risk of flammable vapor escape, thereby increasing the safety of the storage facility.
  • Cost Efficiency: By minimizing product loss through evaporation, secondary seals contribute to cost savings over the operational life of the tank.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Helps storage facilities meet stringent environmental regulations, avoiding fines and ensuring legal compliance.

Applications:

  • Crude Oil Storage Tanks
  • Crude Oil Storage Tanks
  • Chemical Storage Tanks
  • Any Floating Roof Tank Requiring Liquid Level Vapor Control

Technical Specifications:

  •  Material Composition: High-quality materials such as stainless steel, galvanized steel or other corrosion-resistant alloys for structural components, along with durable polymer or elastomeric sealing elements.
  •  Temperature Range: Designed to perform effectively within the temperature ranges typically encountered in oil storage tanks.
  •  Compatibility: Suitable for use with a wide range of petroleum products and chemicals, and compatible with various primary seal designs.
