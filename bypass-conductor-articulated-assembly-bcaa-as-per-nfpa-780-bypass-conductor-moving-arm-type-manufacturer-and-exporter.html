The Agasti Make Bypass Conductor Articulated Assembly (BCAA) / Moving Arm Type Lightning protection System for Floating Roof Oil Storage Tanks is a critical safety component designed to protect floating roof tanks from the damaging effects of lightning strikes. Here's an overview of this product:

Product Overview:

The Agasti Make Bypass Conductor Articulated Assembly (BCAA) / Lightning protection System is designed to safely conduct lightning strikes to the ground, bypassing the tank structure and floating roof. This helps prevent damage to the tank and ensures the safety of personnel and equipment.
