Roof Drain Systems (Articulated Type) Manufacturer

The Agasti Make Roof Drain Joints (Articulated Type) for Floating Roof Oil Storage Tanks are specialized components designed to provide a flexible and reliable seal around roof drain openings in floating roof tanks. Here's an overview of this product:

Product Overview:

The Agasti Make Roof Drain Joints (Articulated Type) are designed to seal the gap between the floating roof and the tank shell around the roof drain openings. These joints are articulated, allowing for movement of the floating roof while maintaining a tight seal, thus preventing vapor emissions and product loss.

Key Features:

  1. Articulated Design: Allows for movement of the floating roof without compromising the seal around the roof drain openings.
  2. Flexible Sealing Material: Made from high-quality, flexible materials that can withstand the harsh conditions within the tank.
  3. Durable Construction: Constructed from corrosion-resistant materials to ensure long-term performance and reliability.
  4. Easy Installation: Engineered for straightforward installation and minimal maintenance, reducing operational downtime.
  5. Compliance with Standards: Meets or exceeds industry standards and regulations for environmental protection and safety.

Benefits

  1. Effective Vapor Control: Prevents vapor emissions and product loss through the roof drain openings, contributing to environmental protection and regulatory compliance

  2. Cost Savings: Minimizes product loss through evaporation, leading to cost savings over the operational life of the tank

  3. Regulatory Compliance: Helps storage facilities comply with environmental regulations, avoiding fines and ensuring legal operation.

  4. Compliance: Helps storage facilities comply with safety regulations and industry standards, avoiding potential fines and ensuring safe operation

Applications:

  1. Crude Oil Storage Tanks

  2. Refind Petroleum Product Tanks

  3. Chemical Storage Tanks

  4. Any Floating Roof Tank Requiring Liquid Level Vapor Control

 

Technical Specifications:

  1. Material Composition: High-quality, corrosion-resistant materials such as stainless steel or other conductive alloys.

  2. Design: Optimized for submerged operation, ensuring effective grounding regardless of the tank's fill level.

  3. Temperature Range: Designed to operate efficiently within the temperature ranges typically encountered in oil storage tanks.

  4. Compatibility: Suitable for use with a wide range of petroleum products and chemicals.
